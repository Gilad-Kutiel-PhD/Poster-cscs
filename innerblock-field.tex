\innerblock{Field}{
A set $F$ with two binary operations $+$ and $\cdot$ ia a \emph{field} if:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $+$ and $\cdot$ are commutative
  \item $+$ and $\cdot$ are associative
  \item $+$ and $\cdot$ have identities, $0$ and $1$ respectively, $0 \neq 1$
  \item every element $a \in F$ has inverse for $+$, written $-a$ 
  \item every element $a \in F$ has inverse for $\cdot$, written $a^{-1}$
  \item $\forall a,b,c \in F,\;  a \cdot (b + c) = a \cdot b + a \cdot c$ 
\end{enumerate}
}
